 November 30 1992 Dear Recently the world watched as the newest space shuttle made its maiden voyage The Endeavor held our attention and focused our imaginations once more upon the stars and renewed our commitment to the knowledge and curiosity that made such a mission possible The IUPUI Challenger Scholars Program encourages bright and dedicated students who want to teach to continue in that career path Four more Challenger Scholars were named in January for the 1992 93 academic year These newest Scholars bring the number to ten ten who like Christa McAuliffe will touch the future Joanne Warner the second Challenger Scholar wrote recently that she teaches second grade at Arlington Elementary in Franklin Township Life is hectic yet very rewarding Joanne is making a difference in the lives of her students The IUPUI Challenger Scholars are chosen from among outstanding students in the humanities sciences technologies and education who have chosen to pursue careers in teaching at any level You can see from the enclosed newspaper article the quality and diversity of those who receive this honor Your past support of the Challenger program has been critical to its success Each award involves a tuition fee remission and a 1000 stipend These have been accomplished through the generosity of individuals like yourself in combination with some campus and school allocations Please renew your commitment today Sincerely Michael Cohen Professor P S When your gift helps an outstanding student become an outstanding teacher you will know that you too have touched the future
