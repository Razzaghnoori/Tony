 October 1992 Dear Friend of Purdue Why am I writing today to ask you to support Purdue University You and I didn t graduate from Purdue Even though our educational goals and career paths led us elsewhere we each have a special place in our hearts for this outstanding University located on the western bank of the Wabash River Purdue has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember I was raised in West Lafayette As I grew older I realized more and more that Purdue isn t just a state institution it is a public university Moreover it is a world class university Purdue belongs to all of us Hoosier and non Hoosier alike and it will be no better than those of us who love and support it Your gift through the Annual Fund will help Purdue continue its leadership role in higher education Your gift will also count toward the largest campaign for private contributions in the University s history Purdue Vision 21 seeks to raise 250 million in private support to ensure that students of the future will receive an education unsurpassed The campaign will support scholarships and distinguished professorships new and creative research special assistance to minorities new and renovated laboratories increased library acquisitions state of the art telecommunications and more I urge you to think about Purdue and the commitment you can make to help us reach these goals Your contribution large or small through the Annual Fund will carry us one more step toward realizing the Purdue dream for the 21st century Sincerely James C Shook P S A reply card and return envelope are enclosed for your convenience Make your check payable to the Purdue Foundation
