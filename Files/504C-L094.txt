 August 25 1993 Name Address City ST Zip Dear Name Things are bustling at Herron in preparation for the new school year Contracts are being signed by faculty room assignments are being made for classes everything s being cleaned and the Gallery is getting ready for the opening of Public Figures We are expecting about 470 students majoring in art and another 1 000 who are taking courses with us but majoring in another area Then there are the continuing education and the alumni sketch classes With all the activity these facilities will be used more than ever As you may know the State Assembly voted to not fund any capital projects for the IUPUI campus The Herron project will be deferred to the 1995 budget requests making it necessary for us to remain at the current site until at least 1998 A number of repairs and alterations will have to be completed at the present facility to assure their usability for a few more years Therefore this year s annual operating campaign for the Herron School of Art and Gallery is focusing on the need for funds to help maintain the facility and our programs We are requesting that you consider making an increased gift to Herron this year in a time of particular need It will go toward the general operating funds of the School or the Gallery as you choose We will certainly appreciate any gift you can make Feel free to call me at any time for additional information about Herron Please include the enclosed form with your contribution Sincerely William J Voos Dean
