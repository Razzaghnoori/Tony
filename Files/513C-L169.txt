 May 28 1991 Dear Classmate I know most of you will find it hard to believe but we are about to celebrate our 35th Anniversary I am just amazed when I see one of you at a dental meeting you just don t look like you have been in practice that long But time is marching on and I know all of you will want to make plans to attend the 1991 IU Fall Dental Conference We had a great time five years ago in Bloomington but due to the remodeling of the Union Building on the Bloomington Campus the conference this year is being held in Indianapolis You will soon receive information from the Alumni Association with the details This year our class is being challenged by the other reunion classes to raise funds The goal is to equip ten new operatories at the dental school The deadline for donations from each class will be July 31 There will be winners in two categories 1 the Class that contributes the most money and 2 the Class that has the greatest percent of class member participation A gold plate will be placed in each operatory to honor the reunion classes Contributing members of the winning classes will receive an added bonus of a tuition waived registration to a nonrestricted CE Course at the dental school during the 1991 92 academic year This is a great opportunity for the Class of 1956 to show who is really number one Best regards Hall D D S P S Enclosed is a pledge card and return envelope to assist in making your tax deductible contribution for the Class of 56
