 Dear IUSD Colleague Indiana University and the health profession suffered a significant loss with the passing of Dr Steven L Bricker on March 21 1994 A nationally recognized innovator in Oral Diagnosis and Oral Medicine Steve is remembered as a gentleman whose care and concern for his students friends and colleagues was warm and genuine Dr Bricker pursued excellence for his department university and profession with unending determination As Chair of Diagnostic Sciences for Indiana University School of Dentistry Steve Bricker set a standard for excellence to which we all aspire We have created a scholarship endowment which will permanently recognize Dr Steven Bricker s contributions to our school and profession The Bricker Scholarship will provide support for students engaged in graduate and post graduate work in the diagnostic sciences at our dental school This endowment is a fitting tribute to a man who made a difference and will realize one of Steve s passions supporting students The outcry from across the country over the loss of Steve Bricker has been significant People here at the school have asked for the opportunity to support students and recognize his good work by participating in the Steven L Bricker Scholarship Endowment You may use the attached form if you wish to make a gift Your contribution made through Indiana University Foundation is tax deductible Sincerely STEVEN L BRICKER SCHOLARSHIP ENDOWMENT
