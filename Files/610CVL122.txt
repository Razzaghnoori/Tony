Foursquare design built in 1917 but has not been owned by the Clinton family for the past 30 years The house is now owned by the non profit Foundation For his first four years Bill Clinton lived with his maternal grandparents Eldridge and Edith Grisham Cassidy at 117 South Hervey in Hope It was in this house that Bill spent his formative years learning to walk talk laugh play read and pray The
