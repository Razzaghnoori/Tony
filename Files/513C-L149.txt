 November 10 1993 Name Address City ST Zip Dear Name You are probably aware of the periodontal scholarship fund at the dental school under my name This is administered through the Indiana University Foundation An award of 500 00 is given yearly to a graduate student in each class who excels in scholarship The graduate program has now been extended to three years which entails another cash award In addition to this award the fund pays for affiliate membership in the Academy as well as providing the Journal of Periodontology for each student With the additional year of training our financial obligation has increased by a third Payment of these awards is made from interest and dividends generated by the principal As you are aware interest income and dividends have dropped considerably prompting this letter You will find a contribution form from the IU Foundation If you have or have not yet contributed to the foundation this year I am asking you to consider doing so Please fill out the enclosed form indicating that this contribution is to be awarded to the Henry M Swenson Periodontic Scholarship Fund As with all contributions made through I U Foundation your gift is tax deductible according to federal and state laws It will most certainly be appreciated by the recipients as well as the foundation Sincerely yours Henry M Swenson
